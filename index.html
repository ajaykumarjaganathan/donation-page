The Project

What I'm Building

I'm Ajaykumar Jaganathan — a solo developer building a full-stack attendance and monitoring system that bridges the gap between students, workers, and administrators.

This app uses autonomous monitoring technology to track attendance in real time — no manual registers, no missing data. Whether it's a classroom or a worksite, the system adapts.

Right now, I'm building this with zero budget. Every rupee you donate goes directly into hosting, development tools, APIs, and making this dream real.
